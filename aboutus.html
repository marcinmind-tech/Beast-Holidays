About Beast Holidays

Travel Beyond Destinations

At Beast Holidays, every journey is planned to be comfortable, memorable and stress-free. From family vacations and honeymoon escapes to pilgrimage tours and group travel, we help travellers explore South India with confidence and convenience.

Your trusted travel partner from Coimbatore.

Beast Holidays is a Coimbatore-based travel company focused on creating smooth travel experiences for individuals, families and groups. With carefully maintained vehicles, flexible planning and destination knowledge, we make every trip easier from start to finish.

Whether you are planning a weekend getaway, hill station retreat, spiritual pilgrimage or multi-day vacation, our team helps you choose the right vehicle, route and travel plan.
